About Me

Hello, I'm Furkan. I am 24 years old and I come from Turkey. After studying Business in Turkey for 2 years, I came to Belgium. I'm currently studying programming at Thomas More University. This is my first year and I'm excited for the coming years.

I am very excited to learn new things and do new projects in this episode. I am aware that I need to evaluate this training very well for new breakthroughs in the future.

History

I was born in 1998 in Samsun, Turkey.I started primary school in 2006 I went to primary and secondary school in Bafra. I won the military high school in high school exams.

In 2012, I moved to Istanbul to study high school. I studied high school for a total of 5 years, including 1 year of preparatory school and 4 years of normal education.

After high school, I started to study business administration at Marmara University in Istanbul in 2019. After 1 year of university life, I moved to Belgium in 2020. I spent my first two years here learning Dutch. After 2 years of language education, I started to study programming at Thomas More University in 2022.

My Plans

This year and next year, I am planning to complete my bachelor's degree at the end of 2 years by reinforcing my knowledge with university education.

In the future, I want to work as a front-end developer in numerous and large companies. That's why I want to specialize especially in HTML-CSS-JavaScripts.In addition to my university education, I aim to develop my programming and algorithm skills from many additional sources.

Finally, I want to see myself in other job positions by learning other programming languages besides Front-end developer in my business life.
